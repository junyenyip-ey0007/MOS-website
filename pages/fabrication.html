Why fabrication is a critical part of your manufacturing process in I4.0?

One of the key benefits of I4.0 is the increased connectivity and integration of different stages of the manufacturing process, from design to fabrication to distribution. Fabrication is a critical component of this process, as it involves the actual production of the physical product. By integrating advanced technologies such as automation and robotics into the fabrication process, manufacturers can improve efficiency, reduce errors, and increase productivity.

Overall, fabrication plays a crucial role in I4.0, as it is an essential part of the manufacturing process that can be optimized and enhanced through the integration of advanced technologies.
