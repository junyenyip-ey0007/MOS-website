Faster time-to-market

PES can help businesses accelerate the product development process, reducing time-to-market and enabling them to stay ahead of the competition.

Reduced costs

Our Expertise

Silicon validation is typically performed using a combination of hardware and software tools that simulate the chip's behavior and test its functionality. The process involves testing the chip's input and output signals, power consumption, timing characteristics, and other performance metrics.

Silicon validation is a crucial part of the chip development process, as it helps to identify and correct any design or manufacturing issues before the chip is released to the market. This process helps to ensure that the chip meets the required quality standards and can perform reliably in the intended application.
