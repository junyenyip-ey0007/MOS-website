What are some of the key components of AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a complex field that involves a combination of different components. Some of the main components of AI include:
Machine Learning

It is a subfield of AI that allows machines to learn from data, identify patterns and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed.

Natural Language Processing (NLP)

It involves the use of algorithms to understand and process human language, both written and spoken.

Computer Vision

It's enabling computers to interpret and understand visual information from the world around us, including images and videos.

Robotics

It involves the use of robots to perform tasks autonomously and interact, using AI algorithms to make decisions and navigate.

Knowledge Representation

It involves the creation of models that can store and manipulate information, allowing machines to reason and make informed decisions.

Planning and Optimization

It involves the use of algorithms to find the best possible solution to a given problem, taking into account various constraints and objectives.

Nerve Platform (coming soon)

Nerve platform is an Industrial IOT (IIOT platform) that consists of systems for connecting frontline industrial processes with backend information systems. They include hardware and software and enable organizations to gain insights and improve operational efficiency from new applications. This platform is align to Industry 4.0.

The Nerve platform allow a direct line between cloud-based or on-premise storage and compute resources and the shop floor. By connecting the sensors and machines (legacy and new) that collect data during production runs, they create a direct line between the places and times when data is collected, data is streamed into a structured time series database and the tools needed for visualization/analysis for various stakeholders from newly developed applications.
